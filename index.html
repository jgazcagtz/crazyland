🛠️ Help & Support

Need assistance? Here's more information about CrazyLand:

Game Mechanics:

  • Swapping Tiles: Click on any emoji and then on an adjacent one to swap them.
  • Matching: Creating a horizontal or vertical line of three or more identical emojis will clear them from the board.
  • Special Emojis: Matching four or more emojis creates special emojis with unique abilities.

Power-Ups:

  • 🔄 Shuffle: Randomly rearranges all emojis on the board.
  • 💣 Bomb: Clears a selected emoji and its surrounding tiles.
  • 📏 Clear Row: Removes an entire row from the board.
  • 📐 Clear Column: Removes an entire column from the board.
  • ✨ Combo Power-Up: Combines multiple power-ups for a larger clearing effect.

Strategies:

  • Plan your moves to create chains and combos for higher scores.
  • Use power-ups strategically to overcome difficult levels.
  • Focus on clearing special emojis to maximize their effects.

If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, feel free to reach out to our support team!
