🎮 How to Play CrazyLand

Master the art of matching and become a CrazyLand champion!

🎯 Basic Rules

  • Match 3 or more identical emojis in a row or column
  • Click adjacent tiles to swap them
  • Create matches to score points and clear tiles
  • Complete levels by reaching the target score

⚡ Scoring System

  • Base Score: 10 points per matched tile
  • Combo Bonus: +5 points per combo level
  • Level Progression: Score 500 × level to advance
  • Time Bonus: Extra points for quick matches

🎮 Game Controls

  • Mouse/Touch: Click tiles to select and swap
  • Pause: Press ESC or click pause button
  • Restart: Press R or click restart button
  • Sound: Toggle audio with sound button

🏆 Tips & Tricks

  • Plan ahead: Look for potential matches
  • Create combos: Chain matches for bonus points
  • Watch the timer: Manage your time wisely
  • Use moves efficiently: Don't waste moves on impossible matches
